Smart LATEX Resumes with AI

Enter your details and a preview your resume will be generated live on your right side. Using a laptop is recommended.

Steps to Generate Your Resume:

  • Step 1: Fill in your personal information.
  • Step 2: Copy the LaTeX code.
  • Step 3: Paste it in a LaTeX editor like Overleaf and Compile.
  • Step 4: You can use the integrated AI assistant to enhance your resume!
